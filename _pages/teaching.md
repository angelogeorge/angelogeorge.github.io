## Instructor

### PHYS 2300 — Electricity & Magnetism Laboratory
*Saint Mary's University, Halifax, NS — 2023 to 2025*

Second-year B.Sc. Physics laboratory. Taught the standard set of E&M
experiments (Coulomb's law, capacitors, RC/RL/RLC circuits, induction,
oscilloscopes), led pre-lab tutorials, supervised data acquisition, and
graded weekly reports.

### PHYS 2400 — Thermodynamics Laboratory
*Saint Mary's University, Halifax, NS — 2023 to 2025*

Second-year B.Sc. Physics laboratory. Calorimetry, gas-law experiments, and
heat-engine demonstrations. Same teaching style as above — hands-on
supervision plus problem-solving sessions.
